# ⚡ Sparked — Mythic abilities for your weapons & armor.

Sparked replaces generic enchantments with **Mythic Artifacts** — single-tier ultimate abilities that turn every fight, every mine, and every moment into a cinematic event. No Level 1/2/3 grind. Just raw, legendary power.

---

## 🗡️ Swords

**Berserker's Wrath** — Right-Click to enter a blood-rage. Gain Strength & Haste for 5s while your vision clouds and damage taken increases. Comes with a rhythmic heartbeat SFX.

**Void Dash** — Right-Click to warp forward in a trail of dragon breath, blinding and damaging every enemy in your path. Includes camera-shake on impact.

**Thunder Strike** — Right-Click to charge your blade with static. Your next hit detonates a massive lightning burst with explosive damage.

**Cloud** — Jump while holding this sword (or wearing the boots) to spawn crystalline snow paths beneath your feet. Completely negates fall damage.

---

## 🪓 Axes

**Shockwave** — Right-Click to slam the ground and send a violent kinetic wave 8 blocks outward. Sends enemies flying with seismic force.

**Combite** — Every consecutive hit on the same target stacks a +10% damage multiplier. Miss once and it resets.

---

## ⛏️ Pickaxes

**Tunnel Vision** — Right-Click to upgrade your mining area to **3×3** for 15 seconds. Live Action Bar countdown included.

**Vein Miner** — Break one ore and the entire vein explodes into your inventory. Supports both regular and deepslate ores.

---

## 🔱 Tridents

**Tsunami** — Throw to project a massive torrent of oceanic force with a visible water wake trail, pulling enemies into the tide.

**Storm Call** — Right-Click to summon a localized storm vortex that sucks nearby enemies into the eye while dealing constant damage.

---

## 🏹 Bows & Crossbows

**Freeze Arrow** — Arrows and rockets leave a snowflake trail and instantly freeze targets on hit. Shattering glass SFX + blindness included.

---

## 🛡️ Armor

**Iron Fortress** — Right-Click to surround yourself in a high-resistance aura. Movement slows to a crawl, but you become near-impenetrable.

**Phantom Cloak** — Right-Click to vanish in a cloud of smoke. Gain invisibility and an absorption shield. Attacking breaks your cover.

---

## 📜 Commands

| Command | Description | Permission |
|:---|:---|:---|
| `/sparked menu` | Opens the Artifact Codex & Virtual Forge | `sparked.public` |
| `/sparked books` | Gives every Mythic Artifact book | `sparked.admin` |
| `/sparked givebook <player> <type>` | Gives a specific artifact book to a player | `sparked.admin` |
| `/sparked reload` | Reloads `config.yml` | `sparked.admin` |

---

## 🛠️ Configuration

Everything is tunable in `config.yml`:
- **Cooldowns** — Set exact recharge times per ability
- **Multipliers** — Adjust damage, duration, and radius
- **Messages** — Full MiniMessage support with gradients & glow

---

## 📋 Compatibility

- **Minecraft**: 1.21.1+
- **Platforms**: Spigot, Paper, Pufferfish, Folia
- **Dependencies**: None
- **Items**: All vanilla materials + custom resource pack items

---

> 💡 **Tip:** Use `/sparked menu` to open the **Mythic Forge** — apply artifacts via a virtual anvil anywhere in the world, no crafting table needed.
